Norton Commando Bikes

The Norton Commando is a classic motorcycle with a rich heritage and iconic design. Known for its exceptional performance and timeless style, the Commando has been a favorite among motorcycle enthusiasts since its introduction.

Features

  • Engine: 961cc parallel-twin
  • Power: 80 hp @ 6500 rpm
  • Torque: 90 Nm @ 5200 rpm
  • Transmission: 5-speed manual
  • Frame: Steel tubular frame with integral oil tank
  • Brakes: Dual front disc and single rear disc
  • Suspension: Adjustable front and rear suspension
